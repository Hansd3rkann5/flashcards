Name the 3 zones of an universal screw and describe their task.

What is a goal that you aim for regarding the wall thickness of a Injection Molding part and why is that?

Uniform wall thickness for one part to avoid different cooling times (thicker - longer | thinner - short) which would lead to

  • warpage
  • sink marks
  • uneven flow resistance → incomplete filling, weld lines, quality variations
